These are the rules for the rules, a server constitution, if you will.
Staff are to abide by these policies when enforcing and updating rules.
Retroactive enforcement of rules.

If a new rule is created to cover a situation not currently covered, it shall not be applied to any offenses before its creation, unless in a situation where serious harm is posed to the server, or members thereof.

Application of rules to related Discord servers.

The rules outlined in this document will apply to any Discord server related to Horizon's end A server would be related to Horizon's End if it meets any of these criteria:
It sources its members from the Horizon's End community.
It represents an organization (Settlement, Nation, etc.) from Horizon's End. , in public facing channels.
Exceptions to this policy may be made in cases of severe harassment or xenophobia.

Enforcement limits of rules.

Most rules should have a statute of limitations, after which, the offense cannot be punished.
This policy is aimed to prevent blackmail and scraping up other's histories.

Conduct Rules

Rules relating to how players conduct themselves in any communication channel

  1. Respect All Players

    We take our player's privacy and safety very seriously. Any threat of malicious actions such as harm, exposure of personal information, or DDOS attacks will be met with an immediate ban. Any violation of Discord's policies will be reported directly to Discord.

  2. No Hateful Conduct

    Hate speech, homophobia, transphobia, racism, sexism, ableism, defamation, and other forms of harassment will not be tolerated. This includes the use of racial, ethnic, or homophobic slurs. Constant petty insults that create a toxic atmosphere will also not be allowed. Punishments will be situational, but we reserve the right to ban a user should we judge the incident to be severe. Server staff may not be aware that something is considered hate speech, in this instance please open a report. (See: Note 3)

  3. No Spamming

    Refrain from posting repeat messages, excessive capitalization, excessive emotes, excessive arguments, and tagging people unnecessarily. Make a reasonable effort to use channels for their respective purposes. If you aren't sure of a channel's topic, check its description. Additionally, English is the only language to be used in public channels. Most players here only understand English, as do the moderators. Please keep it to private messages with others who may understand.

  4. No Sexual/Adult Content

    We do not allow any Sexual content, we have no channels in which such content would be appropriate or allowed. Sexual/Adult content includes nudity, gore, etc. If you are unsure if it is considered NSFW/suggestive. Discussion of hard drugsPsychedelics, opiods, etc. is also prohibited.

  5. No Advertising

    This is not the place for you to promote your server. This does not mean talking about other servers is outright banned. However, if you are slandering or found to be subtly attempting advertisement, moderators will take action. This rule also applies to other servers, advertisement of Horizon's End in any community where it is not allowed will be considered a violation of this rule.

  6. Do not misrepresent rules

    Players must not purposely fabricate, misrepresent, or otherwise give other players a false impression of the server rules. A player who received punishment for a violation that they were unfairly manipulated into via intentional misrepresentation on the part of another player shall have their punishments reversed unless there is compelling reasoning not to do so.

General Rules

Rules that apply in all situations, through gameplay or communication channels.

  1. No Harassment

    Actions aimed at making the server unplayable, or a significantly worse experience, for another player will not be tolerated. These actions include, but are not limited to, placing automatic defenses near another player's base, building obstructions around someone's settlement, or repeatedly killing them once all value from doing so has evaporated. Punishments will be situational, but we reserve the right to ban a user should we judge the incident to be severe.

  2. Do Not Bypass Punishments

    Do not attempt to bypass any punishment using glitches, alternate accounts or client modifications.

  3. Alternate Accounts Are Not Allowed

    We do not allow alternate accounts on Horizon's End. If you have a roommate/sibling on the same IP, be sure to clear it up with staff prior to them logging on.

  4. Do Not Steal Designs

    Do not attempt to steal another player's design, including ships / buildings / BDPs, to use for your own purposes. Additionally, if you are granted blueprint rights to a ship, the main contributor of the build is able to revoke your rights at any time for the printing of said ship. If anyone disagrees, staff action will be taken.

Gameplay Rules

Rules specific to the Minecraft Server.

  1. No Hacked Clients or Unfair Mods

    The use of any hacked clients or mods/resource packs that give an unfair advantage over players is forbidden. This includes fly hacks, kill auras, speed hacks, aint-knockback, player highlighters, and x-ray to name a few. Use of hacks or unfair mods will be met with an extensive ban.

  2. Do Not Grief or Steal From Claimed Land or New Players

    Do not grief or steal from another player's claimed land, regardless of access. This includes all containers, builds, and ships within. Additionally, it is recommended you get permission before taking another player's ship from claimed land. Ship ownership overrides that of the territory, this includes the ability to remove the ship from the claim. However the owner of the territory holds the right to take ownership of that ship, regardless of if it is locked. Stealing crates or activating pistons to push crates also falls under this category, when the ship is in claimed land or a trade city. Stealing and griefing the possessions of players with new player protection is not allowed, regardless of the land those contents were left in. Exceptions can be made for abandoned property.

  3. Do Not Kill New Players Unprovoked

    Newer players will have a grace period where they may play without fear of being attacked. If a new player provokes another through combat, they will lose this protection and you may attack them. If a player has a star beside their name in chat, they are considered a new player and you must not provoke them.

  4. Combat restrictions.

    The following are not allowed while you are under attack:

    • Releasing your ship to deny an attacker any reward

    • Causing your own player to die

    • Claiming land

    A player is considered under attack if an attacker has reached within 1000 meters of their player.

  5. No Boosting

    Boosting is the act of giving a player a substantial amount of credits for free in order to help them level up. This system was built with the idea of players working to level themselves. Any offense will be investigated and determined on a case-by-case basis, and we as a team will be determining whether a loophole was used or not.

  6. Ship design restrictions

    To keep within the spirit of Ship vs Ship combat, purposefully awkward ships that are designed only to break the meta will not be allowed. This may include spindly ships that are impossible to hit, abusing the small minimum block counts of shuttles, or anything designed to give an overwhelming advantage at the cost of immersion. This does not apply to immobile ships. Staff discretion is used to determine what is in violation of this rule.

  7. No wanton destruction of planets.

    Players should avoid actions that destroy the natural beauty of planets. Actions that may be covered under this rule maybe include: Large walls, and large scale surface mining.

  8. Do Not Exploit Bugs or Glitches or Mechanics

    Do not attempt to abuse a bug or glitch, known or unknown. If you discover such a feature, promptly report it to the submit a ticket detailing the issue and avoid touching it further. A bug or glitch is any feature that gives you an unfair advantage over other players. Please reference the dropdown menu at the end of this page for a list of known methods of bug abuse.

  9. Respect Other Player's Experience

    Actions that intentionally interrupt the experience of other players are not allowed. This might include trapping a player or interrupting practice sessions.
