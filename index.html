What is Horizon's End?

Horizon's End is not just a Minecraft server; it's an entire galaxy waiting for you to explore and conquer.

Welcome to Horizon's End! It's a custom-coded space Minecraft server where you can pilot your very own movable Starship, take over entire sectors of space, avoid pirates, and use hyperspace to explore the vast galaxy!

The galaxy is a 200k x 200k space map with 17 unique planets to visit. Our server has an active, growing community that is welcoming to all new players. Join a nation or create your own settlement, all with the goal of galactic domination. The possibilities are endless!
