Horizon's End is a custom coded space Minecraft server. Pilot your very own movable starship, take over entire sectors of space, avoid pirates, use hyperspace to explore the vast galaxy!
The galaxy is a sprawling space map, with 18 unique planets and 7 systems to visit. Our server has an active, growing community that is welcoming to all new players.
Join a nation or create your own settlement, all with the goal of galactic domination. The possibilities are endless!
